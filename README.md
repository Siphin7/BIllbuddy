# BIllbuddy
# 💸 BillBuddy AI

**BillBuddy AI** is a smart expense-splitting application that helps groups manage shared expenses easily and fairly. Whether it's a trip, dinner, or outing, the app ensures that everyone ends up paying the same amount without confusion.

---

## 🚀 Features

* 👥 Add multiple participants
* 💰 Track who paid what
* ⚖️ Equal expense distribution
* ⚡ Smart settlement (minimum transactions)
* 💡 AI suggestion for optimized payments
* 📤 Copy/share summary (WhatsApp ready)
* 🎨 Clean modern UI (CustomTkinter)

---

## 🧠 How It Works

1. Add people to the group
2. Enter expenses (who paid and how much)
3. The app calculates:

   * Total expense
   * Equal share per person
4. It determines:

   * Who owes money
   * Who should receive money
5. Generates the **minimum number of transactions** to settle everything

---

## 📊 Example

If:

* A paid ₹500
* B paid ₹200
* C paid ₹100
* D paid ₹300
* E paid ₹400

Total = ₹1500 → Each should pay ₹300

Output:

* B pays ₹100 to E
* C pays ₹200 to A

✅ Everyone ends up paying ₹300
